1. Electric Roller

It can be said that, from the past to the present, all kinds of rollers such as spring rolls, strawberry batches, snails ... have become close friends with women with curly hair and wavy hair. beautiful waves to "fascinating".

And today, these rollers have been improved to a new level into electric rollers.

The strengths of this small device are:

  • Can make hair easier to curl and wavy with faster time
  • Holds curls or waves longer than other classic rollers.
  • Use electricity to create the right temperature, so it does not make hair dry or damaged.

    • To use electric rollers effectively, you can follow these steps:

    – Step 1: Wash your hair thoroughly, let it dry naturally or use a hair dryer.

    – Step 2: Turn on the machine for a period of 5-10 minutes until the light system is red, that is, the roller tubes have reached enough temperature to curl the hair.

    – Step 3: Lift each roller from the power source and start rolling. Next, divide your hair into 2 sections: the top and the bottom.

    – Step 4: Wrap each roll to the end to create the hairstyle you want. And keep the batch for 10-15 minutes.

    – Step 5: Remove the clip, gently remove each roller and gently lift the hair with your hands.

    – Step 6: Apply hair conditioner and keep hair style.

    This method is also very simple and can be done quickly, very suitable to help you style and care for curly hair for weddings, birthday parties ...

    2. Hair Dryer

    This is probably a multi-functional hair beauty tool that many women love to use. Based on different heating methods and operating mechanisms, hair dryers can be divided into 4 types:

  • Ionic hair dryer.
  • Porcelain hair dryers.
  • Tourmaline hair dryer.
  • Titanium hair dryer.

    • Depending on the structure of your hair and the type of hair you want to do, you can choose different types of dryers for the best effect.

    In the matter of hair beauty, a hair dryer offers many advantages:

    - Save time drying hair:

    A common problem with thick and long hair is that it will take a long time for your hair to dry naturally after cleaning your hair with a strong shampoo . Especially in winter, if you leave your hair wet for too long, your body will get cold and affect blood circulation on the scalp.

    Therefore, using a hair dryer is also one of the effective measures to reduce hair drying time and keep the scalp warm in winter.

    However, you should note that the temperature of the hair dryer should not be adjusted at too high a temperature to limit the rapid evaporation of water in the hair strands, which can easily make the hair dry or damaged.

    - Makes it easy to create a variety of hairstyles:

    If you are too busy and do not have much time to go to the hair salon, using a hair dryer is a very effective way to help you create many hairstyles such as straightening or styling curly hair…

    Not only that, with this method, it will also help you save "quite" on the cost of hair beauty.

    However, excessive use of a hair dryer also has many potential harms to hair such as:

    - Weakening of the hair follicles: the hair follicles can be damaged when subjected to excessive heat and at the same time the pores on the scalp also open, increasing the risk of long-term hair loss.

    – Make hair prone to dryness or split ends: Drying hair with too high a temperature also removes moisture from the hair strands and also accelerates the loss of water on the scalp. Therefore, the hair is very easy to dry, split ends.

    – Excessive use of hair dryers also affects hearing : the sound emitted by hair dryers will increase the risk of harm to your hearing, especially for babies.

    Therefore, you should consider how often you use this type of device and avoid using them excessively.

    3. Hair straightener

    Those of you who love smooth straight hair certainly cannot ignore this type of tool, which is a hair straightener.

    It can be said that a hair straightener is one of the optimal solutions for girls who need to straighten their hair in a short time in the following cases: going out, going to a wedding, going to a new year...

    However, the disadvantage of this type of device is that this is probably not a way to straighten hair like pressing for a long time. Even after just 1 wash right after straightening your hair, your hair will return to its original state.

    Some notes below will help you use a hair straightener more effectively and safely:

    – Do not straighten your hair while it is still wet:

    Many of you make the mistake of not drying your hair, you must have used a hair straightener. This will cause great harm to the hair when the hair is still wet and has to be exposed to the high temperature of the hair dryer, causing damage to the hair.

    – It is recommended to adjust the hair dryer to the appropriate temperature:

    Although, using a hair dryer on high heat will help straighten your hair faster. However, this also brings great harm to the hair strands. At that time, the hair strands will become dry and weak and more prone to breakage.

    Therefore, choose a temperature level that is suitable for your hair condition, as well as the temperature outside the environment so as not to harm your hair.

    – Should condition hair before styling:

    This is one of the most important steps to prevent damage and damage to the hair before using a straightener.

    You should apply a small amount of hair conditioner or use hair masks to protect your hair from the effects of hair straighteners.

    4. Hair curler

    For women who love the luxurious and glamorous beauty of curly or wavy hair, a curling iron is indispensable in the wardrobe.

    With just a few simple steps at home, you can create your own wavy wavy hair.

    Currently on the market there are many types of curling irons made from different materials and sizes with different prices, such as:

  • Mini curling iron, with small size.

  • Ceramic hair curler.

    • In addition, you should also choose the type of curling iron with the right diameter for the hairstyle you want to do. In addition, you should choose hair curlers that can adjust the temperature to limit damage to the hair.

    5. Pressing machine
